The Process

6 Steps. Zero Bureaucracy.

From photo or voice to resolution — no offices, no queues, no middlemen.

1

SECURE SIGN IN

Google, Microsoft, Mobile OTP with password, Live Face Recognition via camera oval with scanning beam, or Fingerprint via WebAuthn/FIDO2. Zero biometric data stored on servers.

2

GPS LOCATION DETECTED

Real GPS auto-detects your precise ward, pin code, municipality. Shows animated map with live pin drop and pulse rings. Or choose from all 36 States & 4,841+ municipalities.

3

SNAP PHOTO OR SPEAK

Upload a photo or tap 🎙️ Voice Complaint to speak in any language — Hindi, Marathi, Tamil, English auto-detected. Live transcription shown as you speak. AI analyzes and files the ticket.

4

AI TICKET WITH QR + PDF + WHATSAPP

Formal complaint auto-generated with GPS, ward, severity, blockchain hash. Download an official PDF certificate, generate a QR code for instant tracking, share via WhatsApp deep link, or rate the response.

5

LIVE SLA TIMER BOARD

Every complaint has a live countdown. Green → Yellow → Red. When timer hits zero, the CLAIM button appears instantly for your delay reward. Track every stage on the timeline — officer name, field visit time, action taken.

6

RESOLVE OR GET REWARDED

Complaint resolved? Rate the response with 5 stars. Low rating auto-escalates to Commissioner. Delayed? Auto-apology letter + ₹250–₹5,000 from Citizen Redress Fund. Unresolved 30 days? Auto RTI generated.
